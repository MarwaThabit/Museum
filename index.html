Featured Artifacts

Statue of Horus

Since Feb 2022

This magnificent statue of the falcon-headed god Horus symbolizes protection and royal power, often associated with the pharaohs of ancient Egypt. The statue is carved from sandstone and stands nearly two meters tall, showcasing the detailed craftsmanship of ancient Egyptian artisans.

Greek Vase

Since April 2023

This exquisite Ancient Greek vase showcases intricate designs and reflects the artistry and cultural significance of pottery in ancient Greece. It features scenes from mythology, including depictions of gods and heroes, and is believed to date back to the 5th century BC, illustrating the craftsmanship of the period.

American Pottery

Since Feb 2019

This exquisite pottery piece showcases traditional techniques and designs, reflecting the rich culture and heritage of Native American communities. Made using locally sourced clay, the vibrant colors and intricate patterns tell stories of the tribe's history and connection to the land, embodying their artistic spirit.

Tyrannosaurus Rex

Since Jun 2022

A life-sized replica of a T. rex fossil, offering insights into the prehistoric era and the evolution of dinosaurs. This replica is crafted using high-quality materials to mimic the original fossil, and it helps educate visitors about the anatomy and behavior of one of the most formidable predators of its time.
